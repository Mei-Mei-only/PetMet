About Team Skylark

We are a passionate team of Computer Science students from Gordon College, Olongapo City, Philippines. Our mission is to revolutionize pet adoption through technology, making it easier for loving animals to find their forever homes.

PetMet combines cutting-edge Natural Language Processing with location-based services to create the most intuitive pet adoption platform. We believe every pet deserves a loving home, and every family deserves the perfect companion.
