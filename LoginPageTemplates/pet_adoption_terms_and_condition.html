Welcome to SKYLARK Pet Adoption! These Terms and Conditions govern your use of our pet adoption services. By using our platform, you agree to comply with these terms. Please read them carefully before proceeding with any adoption.

1. Eligibility for Adoption

To adopt a pet through SKYLARK, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be at least 18 years old.
  • Provide valid government-issued identification.
  • Demonstrate the ability to provide a safe, loving, and stable environment for the pet.
  • Agree to a home visit or virtual inspection by SKYLARK representatives, if required.

2. Adoption Process

The pet adoption process involves the following steps:

  • Application: Submit a completed adoption application form.
  • Screening: SKYLARK will review your application and may contact you for additional information.
  • Interview: Participate in an interview (in-person, phone, or virtual) to discuss your suitability as a pet owner.
  • Approval: If approved, you will be notified and provided with further instructions.
  • Adoption Fee: Pay the required adoption fee, which covers vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other medical expenses.
  • Adoption Agreement: Sign an adoption agreement outlining your responsibilities as a pet owner.

3. Responsibilities of the Adopter

By adopting a pet through SKYLARK, you agree to the following responsibilities:

  • Provide proper food, water, shelter, and veterinary care for the pet.
  • Ensure the pet is treated humanely and with kindness at all times.
  • Keep the pet in a safe and secure environment, both indoors and outdoors.
  • Comply with all local laws and regulations regarding pet ownership.
  • Notify SKYLARK immediately if the pet is lost, stolen, or passes away.
  • Do not transfer ownership of the pet to another person or organization without prior written consent from SKYLARK.

4. Adoption Fees

Adoption fees are non-refundable and cover the following expenses:

  • Vaccinations and medical check-ups.
  • Spaying or neutering.
  • Microchipping (if applicable).
  • Administrative costs.

Note: The Adoption Coordinator reserves the right to adjust adoption fees at any time without prior notice.

5. Health and Behavior of the Pet

While SKYLARK makes every effort to ensure the health and well-being of our animals, we cannot guarantee the following:

  • The pet's health, temperament, or behavior after adoption.
  • The accuracy of the pet's age, breed, or medical history.

Recommendation: Adopters are encouraged to consult with a veterinarian shortly after adoption to address any health concerns.

6. Return Policy

If you are unable to keep the adopted pet, you must:

  • Notify SKYLARK immediately.
  • Return the pet to SKYLARK or an approved representative.
  • Do not abandon the pet or surrender it to another shelter without prior approval.

The Adoption Coordinator reserves the right to reclaim the pet if the terms of the adoption agreement are violated.

7. Liability

SKYLARK is not responsible for any damages, injuries, or losses resulting from the adoption or ownership of a pet, including but not limited to:

  • Property damage caused by the pet.
  • Injuries to the adopter, their family members, or third parties.
  • Behavioral issues or health problems that arise after adoption.

Important: Adopters assume full responsibility for the pet's actions and well-being.
